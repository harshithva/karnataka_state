Taste of Karnataka - Mangalorean Cuisine

The Mangalorean cuisine is a special cuisine from Mangalore in Karnataka. If Mangalore has residents who speak Konkani, Kannada and Tulu, one can imagine the variety of dishes their cuisine could have.

One of the specialities of the dishes is that they use coconut extensively in the dishes in the form of grated coconut, coconut paste, coconut milk, etc.

ome of the dishes of Mangalorean cuisine include Kori Rotti, Neer dosa, Patrode, Dukra Maas (a pork dish) and Khali, a country liquor prepared from coconut flower sap. Photo Courtesy: GracinhaMarco Abundo
